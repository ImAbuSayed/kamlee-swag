Aaja ni aaja mainu seene laa ke hold,
Tappda haan sekan vich hoke uncontrolled.
Main takda haan tainu karda na shy,
Udd'da haan asmaan ch touching the sky.
Nachdi ae jadon tu hildi ae soul,
Akhiyan de ishareyan ch kardi control.
Tutte hoye taryan cho labhda haan face,
Roohan da eh rishta apni endless space.
