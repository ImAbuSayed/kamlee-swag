Kach diyan wangan teriyan kardi click clack,
Gutt tera fadke main kade na aawan back.
Surme di dhaar kardi ae sanu kill,
Dil waale sheeshe ch tu kardi ae thrill.
Chubare utte chadh ke tu kardi flash smile,
Tenu vekhan layi main turda endless mile.
Rabb di saun lagge tu ae saddi queen,
Tere bina sunna lagda ae har scene.
